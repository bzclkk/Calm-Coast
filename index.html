We’re excited to announce an important update to how server transfers are now handled across all departments!

Thanks to the efforts of our Co Owner Jack, we now have a dedicated ticket category made specifically for Server Transfers. This change is aimed at simplifying and streamlining the entire transfer process, no matter which department you’re looking to join; including staff.

To access it, either simply type /new in any chat and select “Server Transfer Ticket” from the menu OR you can go to our #ticket-slection channel and click on "Server Transfer Ticket". Once the ticket is created, it will be handled directly by the dedicated team for our server transfers.

This means you no longer need to handle everything in direct messages. Everything related to your transfer will be handled within that one ticket, all in one place, making it quicker and more efficient for both you and our transfer team.

SACRP UNBAN WAVE

Good afternoon everyone,

Today we're here to announce that we've decided to do an unban wave in our Discord. This means that if you know someone that's been banned from the Discord before, they've been unbanned. This will give EVERYONE that has been banned a second chance at SACRP. If you know someone that's been banned in the past, send them an invite to the discord and we'll welcome them back.

However, everyone that's been unbanned will still need to abide by our server rules. If you do any of the following below, you'll be at risk of being banned again:

- CP
- Sexual Harassment
- Hate towards religious groups
- Threats of DDoS

Once again, we're giving everyone a second chance, and make sure you use it wisely!
